Performance

Two Worlds, One CR-V

The All-New CR-V delivers every drive with power and strength. With its all-new engine and high ground clearance, all terrains will be conquered with ease.

Earth Dreams Technology DOHC
i-DTEC Turbocharged Engine

The sophisticated i-DTEC diesel powerplant is an engineering marvel. Honda managed to dramatically reduce overall engine weight but still have the sturdiness required in an engine. The result combines fuel-efficiency with agile and exhilarating drive. Pistons, crankshafts and connecting rods-core engine parts-are smaller and lighter, reducing frictional resistance between components. This improves fuel-efficiency performance. Engine response to acceleration is also quicker, enhancing driving agility.

The 7-Seater Diesel Turbo CR-V comes in three variants that are powered by the DOHC i-DTEC Turbocharged Engine: SX Diesel 9AT AWD, S Diesel 9AT 2WD and V Diesel 9AT.

SOHC i-VTEC Engine With Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The gas variant (2.0 S CVT) is no less advanced, featuring direct-injection technology and Honda's signature Variable Timing Control. It offers maximum efficiency, but with enthusiasm and punch when the need calls for it. In comparison to current engines, it has improved fuel efficiency and output torque through extensive friction reduction measures.

Safety

Safety that keeps you company. Have the confidence to drive anywhere with a slew of advance safety features from the All-New CR-V.


Honda SENSING

Your safety is now a suite of new possibilities. Thanks to Honda Sensing’s exclusive set of intelligent safety and driver-assisted features designed to alert you and keep your focus in check while driving.
(*For SX Diesel 9AT AWD Variant only)

Driver Attention Monitor

The All-New CR-V alerts you with sound and vibrations when it detects that your level of alertness is low.

Anti-lock Brake System (ABS) With Electronic Brake-force Distribution (EBD)

Stay in control even in emergency situations. ABS with EBD prevents your car from skidding even when you brake hard, especially on slippery surfaces, and retain steering control.

Vehicle Stability Assist (VSA)

The All-New CR-V stays stable around corners as VSA keeps understeer and oversteer under control automatically.

Agile Handling Assist (AHA)

Approach every bend with confidence. AHA improves cornering ability by applying brake pressure to individual wheels automatically. This reduces the need for corrective steering.

Hill Start Assist (HSA)

Prevents roll back on an inclined slope when you release your brake and switch to the accelerator pedal.

Walk Away Auto Door Lock

Leave the door unlocked for 30 seconds or walk 2.0 metres away and the doors will lock automatically.
(For SX Diesel 9AT AWD, S Diesel 9AT and 2.0 S CVT Variants only)

Electric Parking Brake (EPB) With Auto Brake Hold (ABH)

Engage and disengage the parking brake with ease. Brakes remain engaged at a stop until you depress the accelerator.

Emergency Stop Signal (ESS)

During emergency braking situations, the ESS automatically activates hazard warning lights to alert drivers approaching from behind.

Seatbelt Reminder

Driver and the front passenger are always reminded to buckle up before every drive.

Dual Front SRS Airbags, Side*, and Side Curtain* Airbags

*For SX Diesel 9AT AWD, S Diesel 9AT and 2.0 S CVT Variants only.

ISOFIX And Child Anchor

Keep your little loved ones secure and safe with ISO FIX anchor points, the global standard for child car seat safety.

Lane Watch Camera

Always be aware of your All-New CR-V’s surroundings as it eliminates blind spots by warning the driver when other vehicles come too close.
(For SX Diesel 9AT AWD, S Diesel 9AT, and 2.0 S CVT variants only)

The All-New CR-V provides a visual aid with multiple views that give you a comprehensive view of what’s behind, that allows you to park properly and safely.
