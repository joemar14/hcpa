Performance

VTEC TURBO: The Peak of True Performance

The heart and soul of the All-New Civic RS Turbo is its VTEC turbocharged engine mated to a responsive Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) with paddle shifters.

Smaller displacement now means more power, thanks to the sophisticated turbocharger. Higher cruising efficiency and robust performance are now the name of the game.

Combined with the latest iteration of VTEC technology, there is no doubt to the sporting intention of this high-performance yet environment-friendly engine.

Safety

Safety is always a priority. Once again, Honda leads the way and ensures that you’ve got nothing to worry about.


Front, Side, and Side Curtain Airbags

Electronic Parking Brake (EPB) with Auto Brake Hold (ABH)

Engage and disengage the parking brake with ease. Brakes remain engaged at a stop until you depress the accelerator.

Emergency Stop Signal (ESS)

Relays the message of panic and emergency stop to drivers behind you. The system flashes the hazard lights as a warning.

Walk Away Auto Lock

The All-New Civic automatically locks all the doors when you have moved more than 2.5 meters away from the car.

Anti-Lock Braking System (ABS) with Electronic Brake Force Distribution (EBS)

Prevent your wheels from locking while braking hard, specially on slippery surfaces, and retain steering control.

Vehicle Stability Assist (VSA)

Keeps your vehicle on its intended path to travel when an understeering or oversteering event occurs.

Hill Start Assist (HSA)

Prevents your vehicle from rolling backward when starting from a stop going uphill.

Ace Body Design

The All-New Civic's engineering dissipates the energy of a crash away from the passenger compartment and minimizes damage to other impacted vehicles.
