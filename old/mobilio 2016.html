Exterior

LOOKS GOOD FROM ANY ANGLE

Cutting-edge design clearly distinguishes the All-New Mobilio as the stylish MUV for the family. The All-New Honda Mobilio leads the way with a contemporary design that fuses form with function.


Front Grille

HEADLIGHTS

The headlight has a tear drop shaped from front to side corners for heightened function and style.

ALLOY WHEELS

Lighter 15" alloy wheels are provided for a faster pace, making it flexible yet firmer on the ground. Its shine and colour enhance an appealing look.

FOG LIGHTS

The front fog lights provide better illumination to increase range of visibility in the rain, dust, and fog. (1.5 RS NAVI and 1.5 only)

SIDE TURN SIGNAL

MICROTYPE ANTENNA

Microtype antenna is mounted for better signal reception and clearer frequency selection.

TAIL LAMP

SLEEK SHOULDER LINE

Interior

SEATS OF FUN

With enough space for leg room and head room, the All-New Honda Mobilio is the ultimate fun family vehicle by giving comfort and convenience right at your seats.


ACCESSIBILITY TO CONTROLS

The All-New Honda Mobilio’s Advanced Technology makes driving experience effortless through its accessible command points making room for easier maneuvering and better over-all vehicle handling.

FLEXIBLE SEATS FOR 7 SMILES

The All-New Honda Mobilio’s well-crafted interiors makes enough room for 7, both kids and adults to fit inside the MUV, to make sure your journey is filled with 7 smiles. With this first 7-seater MUV from Honda – no one gets left behind!

FLEXIBLE INTERIORS

The one-touch-fold-to-tumble function allows wider room for easier passenger ingress and egress.

FLEXIBLE LUGGAGE SPACE

The All-New Honda Mobilio’s flexible interior include seats that tumble forward or fold down easily with one touch mechanism into different configurations. All-New Mobilio’s large trunk space make storage easy. Once 3rd row seats are tumbled, maximum cargo space for luggage, gear, and other stuff is provided for your convenience.

CONVENIENCE AS YOU PLEASE

The All-New Honda Mobilio cargo space allows travelers to store more of their essentials as they go on the road. With its large trunk space, you will no longer have to worry about forgotten luggage.

KEYLESS ENTRY

Electronic lock for better control access to your car.

INSTRUMENT CLUSTER

The instrument cluster is presented in a striking 3D appearance for optimal visibility that gives the driver a comprehensive view of the information he needs.

STEERING WHEEL AUDIO CONTROLS

Steering Wheel Audio Controls allows easy selection of audio functions without taking your eyes off the road. (1.5 RS NAVI and 1.5V only)

Econ Button

TILT ADJUSTABLE STEERING WHEEL

Tilt Adjustable Steering Wheel allows the driver to set the steering wheel to a comfortable driving position.

REAR AIRCON

Rear aircon vents with adjustable fan speed provide good ceiling comfort for back seat passengers.

2-DIN AUDIO WITH BLUETOOTH, CD, USB/AUX PORTS

The stereo features various onboard entertainment options for more pleasurable drive. (1.5V only)

CONVENIENT STORAGE SPACES

Cup holders are accessible for both driver and passengers with ample size to hold large drinks. Center console and inner door panels provide bottle holders.

Performance

POWERFUL YET EFFICIENT

The All-New Honda Mobilio is equipped with a 1.5 liter i-VTEC engine with CVT to optimize fuel efficiency without compromising pulling power.

1.5 Liter i-VTEC ENGINE

Ensures good pulling power without that accelerating engine noise. The i-VTEC engine technology intelligently delivers powerful yet fuel efficient driving performance.

Power : 120 ps at 6,600 rpm
Torque: 14.8 kg-m at 4,800 rpm

Continuous Variable Transmission (CVT)

A lightweight and compact CVT offers seamless shifting for smooth and powerful acceleration that enhances driving performance.

Developed under Honda’s Earth Dreams Technology, the CVT also works to deliver efficient fuel economy.

ECO Lamp

A strong visual cue that lights up when you engage in efficient driving style, assisting you to cover more ground.

Safety

SAFETY IS OUR PRIORITY

The All-New Honda Mobilio ensures your peace of mind on the road with safety features equipped for all variants.


DUAL SRS AIRBAGS

Ensuring driver and passenger safety, it comes with Dual SRS Airbags to protect occupants in the event of collision.

EDRIVER SEATBELT REMINDER

Warns the driver to buckle up once driving.

ISOFIX

ISOFIX provides standardized anchoring points for the child safety seat, to guarantee complete security for the youngest member of the family.

ANTI-LOCK BRAKING SYSTEM (ABS)

This safety feature helps maintain contact with the road surface while braking to avoid uncontrolled slipping. Through a responsive threshold braking and cadence braking, improved control is attained effortlessly.
