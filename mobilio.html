Bold Design, Bold Features

The New Mobilio is designed to look good and make the passengers feel good. Its interior trimmings are sleek and stylish. The passenger features are convenient. It has all the benefits for all seven people inside the New Mobilio.


Flexible Interior

The New Mobilio’s well-crafted interiors make enough room for seven persons, both kids and adults. Now that’s a good-sized MUV. The one-touch fold-to-tumble function allows wider legroom for easier passenger ingress and egress.

Flexible Luggage Space

The New Mobilio’s flexible interior includes seats that tumble forward or fold down easily with a one-touch mechanism. The large trunk space makes storage easy. Once 3rd row seats are tumbled, maximum cargo space for luggage gear, and other necessities is provided for your convenience.

Performance

MUV With Power and Efficiency

The New Mobilio is equipped with a 1.5 liter i-VTEC engine in Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) to optimize fuel efficiency without compromising pulling power.

1.5 Liter i-VTEC ENGINE

The i-VTEC engine technology intelligently delivers powerful yet fuel efficient driving performance.

Power: 120 ps at 6,600 rpm
Torque: 14.8 kg-m at 4,600 rpm

Continuous Variable Transmission (CVT)

A lightweight and compact CVT offers seamless shifting for smooth and powerful acceleration that enhances driving performance.

Developed under Honda’s Earth Dreams Technology, the CVT also works to deliver efficient fuel economy.

ECO Lamp

A strong visual cue that lights up when you engage in efficient driving style, assisting you to cover more ground.
