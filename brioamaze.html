Performance

Efficiently Powerful (1.3 i-VTEC 4-Cylinder Engine)

The All-New Brio Amaze's powertrain provides superior performance and efficiency. The 1.3L i-VTEC 4-Cylinder engine is standard across the model range, and generates a class-leading 100 ps and 13 kg-m. From low to high revs, it delivers power promptly as needed.

Safety

Keep You Protected.

Drive on knowing that The All-New Honda Brio has advanced safety features to ensure your well-being and peace of mind. These active and safety systems work in tandem to prevent road mishaps, or to limit injury when accidents do happen.


Dual SRS Airbags

Because your safety is paramount, the All-New Honda Brio Amaze is equipped with high-level safety technologies. Dual SRS airbags are standard across the model range. Vehicle security is covered by electronic protective systems.

G-Con

Specially constructed frame dissipates G-forces in the event of a collision.

Three-Point ELR Seatbelts

Pretensioners and load limiters minimize chest motion and compression.

Impact-Mitigating Headrests

Headrests reduce chances of neck injury during low-speed rear impact.

Anti-Lock Braking System (ABS)

ABS is fitted in all variants of the All-New Honda Brio Amaze. By regulating brake pressure during emergency braking situations on slippery surfaces, the system prevents the wheels from locking up and allows you to retain control of the vehicle.
